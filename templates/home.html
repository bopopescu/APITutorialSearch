Six Basic Models

There are six basic models for API tutorial intelligent search based on
text retrieval and natural language processing.

TF-IDF

The tf-idf is the product of two statistics, term frequency and inverse document frequency.

LDA

It is a generative statistical model that allows observations to be explained by unobserved groups.

LSI

It identifies patterns in the relationships between the terms and concepts in unstructured text.

Word2Vec

Word2vec is a group of related models that are used to produce word embeddings.

TF-IDF + Word2Vec

It is a combination of word2vec and tf-idf, using the tf-idf weighted embedding vectors.

LSTM

It is an artificial recurrent neural network, architecture used in the field of deep learning.
